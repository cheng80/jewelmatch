# Charged Arcane QA

Regenerated with complementary effect colors per gem: red->cyan, blue->gold/pink, yellow->blue/cyan, green->magenta, purple->lime/gold, orange->cyan/blue. Row 1: star colors 1-6 with compact engraved sunburst and small core. Row 2: supernova colors 1-6 with restrained internal ring and brighter core. Effects are clipped to gem alpha and avoid UI-like white line overlays.

Yellow charged frames adjusted to high-contrast violet/magenta complement for readability.
